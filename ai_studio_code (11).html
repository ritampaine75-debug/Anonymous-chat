1. App Introduction

Welcome to the Terminal Chat Node. This is a purely anonymous real-time communication tool designed for privacy and speed.

  • No Login Required.
  • No Email or Phone Numbers.
  • No Passwords.
  • Just enter a nickname and chat.

2. Privacy & Data Policy

We respect your anonymity. Here is how the system handles data:

  • Nickname: Temporary usage only.
  • Messages: Stored in real-time cloud database for history.
  • Auto-Delete: Rooms are automatically wiped after 48 hours of inactivity.
  • No Tracking: We do not track IP addresses or browser fingerprints.

3. Terms & Conditions

By accessing the Terminal, you agree to:

  • Do NOT share illegal content or malware.
  • Do NOT share personally identifiable information (PII).
  • Maintain a respectful environment (though we do not moderate).
  • You are solely responsible for your interactions.

4. How to Use

  1. Click the [ GO TO CHAT ] button below.
  2. Enter a unique Nickname when prompted.
  3. You will join the Global Public Lobby automatically.
  4. Type and press Enter to send.

5. Private Chatrooms

You can create secret channels by modifying the URL.

Example:

chat.html?room=secretbase

Share the full URL with your friends. Anyone with the link can join that specific room. If the room doesn't exist, it is created instantly.

6. System Features

  • History: Recent messages are persistent until room expiry.
  • Live Presence: See how many users are currently connected.
  • Delete: You can delete your OWN messages within the same session.
  • System Logs: Notifications when users connect or disconnect.
